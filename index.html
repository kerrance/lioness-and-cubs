About Rubie

Level 3 Pre & Post Natal Qualified • 10+ Years Experience

Hi, I'm Rubie - the founder of Lioness and Cubs.

I'm a Level 3 Pre and Post Natal qualified trainer with over 10 years of experience in the fitness industry and I've had the pleasure of working with hundreds of women throughout their fitness journeys. Being a mother myself, I know first hand the physical and emotional changes that come with pregnancy, postpartum recovery, and motherhood beyond.

Lioness and Cubs was built on one powerful belief: mums are incredibly strong - sometimes they just need reminding.

My classes focus on creating solid foundations. During pregnancy, we work safely and intentionally to keep your body strong whilst it adapts to your growing baby bump. Post-partum, we rebuild with care, restoring core strength, improving mobility, and developing functional strength that supports you in everyday life.

This isn’t about pressure or unrealistic expectations. It’s about reconnecting with your body, building resilience, and recognising your capability.

I want every mum who steps into my class to feel powerful, to move with confidence, to trust her body, and to leave feeling stronger than when she arrived.

At Lioness and Cubs, we don’t just exercise. We build strength. We build confidence. We build lionesses.

I can’t wait to welcome you.

What to Expect

Do I need to bring anything?

Just bring a yoga mat, a water bottle, and comfortable clothes you can move in. If you don’t have a mat yet, just let Rubie know.

When can I start?

You’re welcome to join at any stage of pregnancy. For postnatal classes, we recommend waiting until at least 6 weeks postpartum and getting clearance from your GP or midwife first.

Are babies welcome?

Yes! Babies are welcome in postnatal and Mums & Cubs classes. They can lie on a mat beside you or sit in a bouncer while you exercise.

What if I’ve never exercised before?

All fitness levels are welcome. Classes are designed to be adapted to you — whether you’re a seasoned gym-goer or this is your very first fitness class, you’ll feel right at home.
